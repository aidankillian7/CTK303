I am interested in many aspects of technology from creative and technical aspects. I would always tinker with hardware and software to see how things work and make them work the way I wanted.

I write programs, develop games, and an operating system technician. I love writing programs that would assist me in repetitive tasks that require a lot of labor to complete. I also write programs to solve problems and aid in my daily life. Over the years to have learned to inside and out of hardware and operating systems like Windows. For the past few years, I have been learning about Linux systems for desktop and server use.

On the other end from the technical parts of technology, I also dive into the creative aspects of it. I love creating graphics works like photo editing and vector art. I love creating vector logos and single drawings. I am also great at 3D modeling in Blender and some animation. Not only do I like making static art, but also motion pictures. I love to create cinematic films in either real life or in games like GTA5. Editing clips and syncing them to music with timing and flow is also my specialty.

Projects

DarkStar Dogfight

DarkStar Dog is a bullet hell spaceship themed unity game. The game was developed as a Mid term project and then have moved to a Final porject with updated assets and a full boss. The game gets harder as the game progress by increasing the enemys min and max health cap. The game features a score board system where a Web UI was displayed during a game showcase.

Decentralized Multiplayer Framework

The unamed Framework is a idea of a game server framework where when a studio disconinues their game they can hand off the infustrure off to the player base. The players get to decided of the game should stay alive or not.

Dual Of Fate

Dual Of Fate is a Tarot card game that works like a auto battler. The game has 3 lanes and a truth table of what cards can be beat and how. When a player wins a round they can draw a Tarot card that will turn the games rules on its head and add rules like increaseing a power in a certain card to adding another lane. Currently I am working on making the game as a Unity game with full multiplayer capibilities.
